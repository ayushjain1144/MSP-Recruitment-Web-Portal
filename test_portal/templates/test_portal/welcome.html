Rules

  • Exam duration is of one hour.
  • The exam consists of 18 questions divided into two rounds-1 and 2. Round 1 comprises of 7 questions and Round 2 comprises of 8 questions.
  • Marking Scheme is written at the top of every question.
  • All questions in Round 1 are of MCQ type where there may be more than one correct options. Round 2 questions are of subjective type.
  • Make sure that you don't have any unauthorised material (e.g. written notes, notes in dictionaries, paper, and sticky tape eraser) with or near you.
  • Everything you write must be in the rough sheets provided.
  • If you have a question or need more papers, raise your hand and a senior will come to you. Seniors will not give hints or answers, so please do not ask for them.
  • Leave the exam hall quickly and quietly. Remember to take all your belongings with you.
  • Remember! Cheating is not allowed and action will be taken.
